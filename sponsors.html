SCiL is the main venue for research at the intersection of Linguistics and Computer Science. Supporting SCiL is an opportunity to put your corporate name in front of the computational linguists and natural language processing researchers that make up this community.

If you are interested in sponsoring SCiL 2025, please contact the SCiL General Chair (Carolyn Anderson).

SCiL Sponsorship Tiers

Level & Donation      Benefits
Bronze $5,000
  • Logo on website and in proceedings
  • Contact information of attendees who have opted-in to be contacted by sponsors
Silver $8,000
  • Logo on website and in proceedings
  • Contact information of attendees who have opted-in to be contacted by sponsors
  • One complimentary full conference registration
  • Printed materials at registration desk
Gold $15,000
  • Logo on website and in proceedings
  • Contact information of attendees who have opted-in to be contacted by sponsors
  • One complimentary full conference registration
  • Printed materials or other items in conference bag
  • A table in the conference area
Platinum $20,000
  • Logo on website and in proceedings
  • Contact information of attendees who have opted-in to be contacted by sponsors
  • One complimentary full conference registration
  • One complementary main conference registration
  • Printed materials or other items in conference bag
  • A table in the conference area
  • Logo on pre-roll of conference videos
  • Opportunity to speak briefly during the opening session
  • An opportunity to further sponsor one of the following, as available:
    • Lanyards, as supplied by sponsor
    • Welcome reception/poster session sponsor on first day of main conference
    • Social hour sponsor at end of second day of conference
    • Other opportunities as agreed between the sponsor and the conference organizers
